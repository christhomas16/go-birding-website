## 4. Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### Phase 1: Create Designs (Week 1)

**Option A: DIY Design**
1. Use Canva (free) or Adobe Illustrator
2. Create logo-based designs first (easiest)
3. Start with 5-10 core designs
4. Keep it simple - text + logo works great

**Option B: Hire Designer**
- Fiverr: $25-100 per design
- Upwork: $50-200 per design package
- Get 10 designs for ~$300-500

**Design Requirements:**
- 300 DPI minimum for print quality
- PNG with transparent background
- Follow Printful's design guidelines

**Tip:** Start with your app logos and simple text designs. You can always add more complex designs later.

---

### Phase 2: Set Up Print-on-Demand (Week 1-2)

#### Printful Setup

1. **Sign Up**
   - Go to [printful.com](https://printful.com)
   - Create account (free)
   - No credit card required

2. **Upload Designs**
   - Click "Add Products"
   - Choose product (start with Gildan 5000 Unisex T-Shirt)
   - Upload your design
   - Use mockup generator to create product photos

3. **Choose Products**
   - **Must-haves to start:**
     - 2-3 t-shirt styles (unisex, women's fitted)
     - 1-2 hat styles (baseball cap, trucker)
     - Stickers (cheap to produce, easy add-on sales)
   - **Add later:**
     - Hoodies (higher profit, seasonal)
     - Mugs
     - Tote bags
     - Phone cases

4. **Set Up Branding (Optional but Recommended)**
   - Custom packing slips with your branding
   - Add "From Go Birding AI" note
   - Makes unboxing professional

5. **Order Samples**
   - CRITICAL: Order 2-3 samples first
   - Check print quality, sizing, colors
   - Take your own product photos
   - Use 20% discount (up to 3 samples/month)
   - Cost: ~$30-50 for 3 samples

**Time:** 2-4 hours to set up products

---

### Phase 3: Set Up Shopify Store (Week 2)

1. **Create Shopify Account**
   - Go to [shopify.com](https://shopify.com)
   - 14-day free trial (no credit card needed)
   - Choose "Basic" plan when trial ends

2. **Store Setup**
   - Store name: "Go Birding Merch" or "Birding Gear Shop"
   - Choose a theme (free "Dawn" or "Refresh" themes work great)
   - Add your logo
   - Customize colors to match your apps

3. **Install Printful App**
   - Shopify App Store ‚Üí Search "Printful"
   - Install (free)
   - Connect your Printful account
   - Import your products

4. **Add Products to Store**
   - Products auto-import from Printful
   - Write descriptions (focus on benefits, not features)
   - Add tags (birding, birdwatching, etc.)
   - Organize into collections

5. **Set Up Payments**
   - Enable Shopify Payments (avoid extra 2% fee)
   - Add bank account for payouts
   - Set tax settings (automatic)

6. **Configure Shipping**
   - Printful handles all shipping
   - You just set customer-facing rates
   - Suggested: Flat $4.99 shipping or free over $50

7. **Legal Pages**
   - Use Shopify's templates
   - Refund/return policy (Printful handles returns)
   - Privacy policy
   - Terms of service

**Time:** 4-8 hours for full setup

---

### Phase 5: Marketing & Launch (Week 3-4)

1. **Soft Launch to App Users**
   - Email to Go Birding AI users (if you have a list)
   - In-app banner in Bird Chatter
   - Social media announcement

2. **Content Marketing**
   - Blog post: "New Go Birding Merch!"
   - Instagram posts showing products
   - Share mockups on Twitter/X

3. **Influencer Outreach (Optional)**
   - Send free products to birding YouTubers
   - Ask for honest reviews
   - Cost: $50-100 in free merch

4. **Paid Ads (Optional, Later)**
   - Start with $5-10/day on Facebook/Instagram
