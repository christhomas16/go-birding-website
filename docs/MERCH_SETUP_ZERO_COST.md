## Recommended Platforms (Ranked)

### ü•á #1: Redbubble (Best Overall)

**Why It's Great:**
- Largest marketplace with millions of monthly visitors
- 70+ product types (t-shirts, stickers, mugs, phone cases, wall art)
- You set your own profit margins (10-30% typical)
- Completely free, no fees unless you sell
- Easiest to get started

**How It Works:**
1. Upload your design
2. Set your profit margin (e.g., 20%)
3. Redbubble sets the retail price automatically
4. When someone buys, Redbubble prints and ships
5. You get paid your margin via PayPal

**Earnings:**
- T-shirt base price: ~$19
- Your 20% margin: ~$4 per shirt
- Sell 100 shirts = $400 profit
- **$0 monthly costs**

**Drawbacks:**
- Lower margins than Shopify approach
- No control over branding/packaging
- Redbubble platform fees on sales (deducted from your margin)
- You can't build your own brand as easily

## Detailed Setup Guide

### Setting Up Redbubble (30 minutes)

**Step 1: Create Account**
1. Go to [redbubble.com](https://www.redbubble.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" ‚Üí "Sell your art"
3. Enter email, username, password
4. Free account, no payment method needed

**Step 2: Upload Design**
1. Click "Add New Work"
2. Upload PNG file (transparent background recommended)
3. Title: "Go Birding AI Logo T-Shirt"
4. Tags: birding, birdwatching, birds, AI, technology, nature
5. Description: Write compelling copy about your app

**Step 3: Enable Products**
1. Redbubble shows your design on 70+ products
2. Enable the ones you want (t-shirts, stickers, mugs, etc.)
3. Adjust placement/sizing for each product

**Step 4: Set Your Margin**
1. Default is usually 20% (around $4 per shirt)
2. You can increase to 30% (around $6 per shirt)
3. Higher margins = fewer sales (price is higher)
4. Start with 20% to test

**Step 5: Publish**
1. Click "Publish"
2. Your products are now live!
3. Share link: `redbubble.com/people/yourname`

**Earnings:**
- Paid monthly via PayPal
- Minimum $20 threshold
- Typically 30-60 days after sale (production + delivery time)

**Time:** 30 minutes for first design, 10 minutes each additional
